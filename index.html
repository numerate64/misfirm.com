About MIS Solutions

Founded in Boston, built on infrastructure expertise

Established in 1999 in Boston, Massachusetts, MIS Solutions LLC originally operated as MIS Consulting. The firm began as a sole proprietorship serving organizations that needed technology consulting expertise, with early work centered on compute, virtualization, storage, backup, and replication. Today, that foundation extends into cloud services with emphasis on AWS, Microsoft Azure, Microsoft 365, and broader modernization initiatives.
